## 👤 About

**Dr. Ramesh Kumar Yadav** serves as the **Head of Department, Computer Science & Engineering** at **The ICFAI University, Raipur** — a visionary academician, researcher, and mentor who has been instrumental in shaping the department's academic identity, research culture, and technological ambitions.

Under his leadership, the CSE department has:

- 🏗️ Established a **structured open-source presence** on GitHub for the entire university
- 🔬 Driven **faculty and student research** toward real-world impact
- 🤝 Fostered an environment where **students lead, build, and collaborate**
- 🏆 Launched and recognized **student-run technical clubs** that create lasting communities

> *This repository is both a personal academic profile and a tribute to the students, faculty, and clubs that make the CSE department thrive.*

## 🚀 The GitHub Initiative

Dr. Ramesh Kumar Yadav was the **founding force** behind **[The ICFAI University, Raipur's Official GitHub Organization](https://github.com/The-ICFAI-University-Raipur)** — making it one of the few universities in Chhattisgarh with a structured, open-source academic presence.

### 🎯 Initiative Goals

```text
✦  Create a transparent, public record of academic and research work
✦  Give students a professional platform to showcase their projects
✦  Enable collaboration between students, faculty, and industry
✦  Build a living archive of knowledge for future batches
✦  Align the department with global open-source culture
```
